Our Services

At DE-ABDULMETAL GHANA LIMITED, we specialize in the fabrication and installation of high-quality carports, rails, and gates designed to enhance safety, security, and aesthetics for both residenal and commercial properes. Our services are built on precision, durability, and a commitment to excellence.

Carports

We design and construct durable, weather-resistant carports that provide reliable protections for vehicles against sun, rain, and other harsh conditions. Our carports are available in a variety of designs and finishes, offering both functionality and visual appeal to complement your building’s exterior


Rails

Our expertly crafted metal rails and balustrades combine safety with style. We provide custom-made designs suitable for staircases, balconies, walkways, and boundary walls ensuring strong, elegant, and long-lasng result


Gate

We manufacture and install premium-quality gates that balance strength, security, and design. From modern automated gates to tradional manual styles, our products are customized to meet your specific needs and built to withstand years of use while maintaining their aesthetics value.

Carport, Rail & Gate Design


We specialize in creating durable, stylish, and secure metal structures that enhance both function and appearance. From modern carports to custom railings and elegant gates, we design with precision, safety, and aesthetics in mind..

Rail Maintenance & Recoating


Rust removal, repainting, and reinforcement for worn or corroded rails.

Carport Installation


Custom-built carports to protect vehicles from sun, rain, and harsh weather. Available in steel, aluminum, or polycarbonate roofing.

Rail Installation & Welding


Professional installation for staircases, balconies, and outdoor decks. Includes welding, polishing, and finishing.

Gate Fabrication & Design


Modern or traditional designs in steel, aluminum, or wood — tailored for residential or industrial use.

Carport & Gate Maintenance


Keep your carport and gates in top condition with our professional maintenance services. We handle cleaning, lubrication, inspections, and repairs to ensure smooth operation, enhanced safety, and a long-lasting, attractive finish.
